Cameron Boyce

An American Actor

He began his career as a child actor

Here is a timeline for Cameron Boyce

2008
He made his debut in Panic at the disco's music video "That Green Gentleman", and appeared in films such as Mirrors, Eagle Eye and General Hospital: Night shift.
2010-2011
He appeared on Dancing with the stars and The Legion of Extraordinary Dancers
2011-2015
He starred on the hit show Jessie, Grown Ups and Grown Ups 2, and made appearances on Shake it up, and Goodluck Charlie.
2015-2019
Continued his roles in his hit shows, aswell as the film Runt
2019
He died of a seizure at the young age of 20. He became a role model for young actors.
